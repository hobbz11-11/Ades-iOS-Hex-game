How to Play

Hexello and Hexplode (Simple Version)

Game Mode 1: Hexello

Hexello is a bit like Othello/Reversi on a hex board.

Goal: Finish with more tiles of your color than your opponent.

Rules

  1. You can only place on an empty tile.
  2. Your move must trap enemy tiles in a line between your old tile and your new tile.
  3. Trapped tiles flip to your color.
  4. If you have no legal move, your turn is skipped.
  5. If neither player can move, the game ends. More tiles wins.

Examples

  • Example 1: Line shows Red, Blue, Blue, empty. If Red plays on the empty spot, both Blues flip to Red.
  • Example 2: If your move traps tiles in 2 directions at once, both lines flip.
  • Example 3: If it is your turn but there is no legal move, the other player goes again.

Game Mode 2: Hexplode

Hexplode is about building up balls and making chain explosions.

Goal: Take control of the board with smart explosions.

Rules

  1. You can play on an empty tile or on your own tile.
  2. Every move adds 1 level (one more ball).
  3. When a tile goes above level 5, it explodes.
  4. The exploding tile becomes empty.
  5. All neighbor tiles get +1 and become the exploding player's color.
  6. If a neighbor also goes above 5, it explodes too (chain reaction).

Examples

  • Example 1: Your red tile is at level 5. You add 1. It hits level 6 and explodes.
  • Example 2: Two nearby tiles were level 5. They both receive +1 from the blast and also explode.
  • Example 3: A blue neighbor gets hit by your red explosion. It changes to red and gains a level.

Simple Strategy Tips

  • Hexello: Corners are very strong. Try to get them.
  • Hexello: Think one move ahead, not just this move.
  • Hexplode: Build a few strong spots instead of only one big stack.
  • Hexplode: Watch for enemy level-5 tiles that might explode soon.
Tip: If you are learning, use Easy mode first. Then move up to Hard mode when you start winning more often.
