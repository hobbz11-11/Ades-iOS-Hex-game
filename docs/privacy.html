Hex Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 March 2026

Overview

Hex is a strategy game app featuring Hexello, Hexplode, and Hexfection. This app is designed to work without requiring account creation or direct submission of personal information.

Information Collection

Hex does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through the app itself.

Tracking and Advertising

Hex does not use third-party advertising, does not track users across apps or websites, and does not use App Tracking Transparency.

Analytics

Hex does not currently use third-party analytics services to collect usage data from within the app.

On-Device Data

Gameplay settings and app preferences may be stored locally on your device to support the app experience. This information is not used to identify you personally and is not transmitted to the developer by the app.

Children's Privacy

Hex does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
